About me


I am a graphic design major at Massey University in Wellington, New Zealand. Design has been my passion for many years, in that time I have explored many fields such as fine art, graphic design, animation, photography, fashion design, object design and my newly found hobby of leather craft. I like creating work that people can look at for hours, to see hidden intricacies, details, messages and meanings. I like to use my works to say something I feel needs to be said in todays world but I also like creating works for the sake of aesthetics. I have lots to do and learn in the up-coming years so stay tuned for new creations.
